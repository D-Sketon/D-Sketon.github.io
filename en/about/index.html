Why Did I Develop the Hexo Theme Reimu?

In September 2022, I submitted my first fix PR to the Hexo core repository, marking the beginning of my contributions to Hexo. It was from this point that I started to entertain the idea of creating my own Hexo theme. Consequently, in January 2023, I embarked on the development of the Reimu theme. Reflecting back, this decision might seem a bit foolish, as it coincided with the early stages of the spring recruitment season.
